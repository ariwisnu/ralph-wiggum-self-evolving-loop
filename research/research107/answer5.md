## Reasoning

Let me work through each clue.

3. "Yet the farther you stand from me, the warmer you feel." — Here's the catch. For fire (or any heat source), the relationship is the opposite: the *closer* you stand, the warmer you feel; the farther away, the colder. This line is physically inverted. The word "yet" even signals that it is meant as a contrast/twist.

So the first two clues unambiguously point to fire, while the third clue is a deliberate reversal that does not actually hold for fire — or for any normal heat source, since nothing makes you feel warmer the farther away you get.
